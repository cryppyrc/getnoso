Get started with Noso

Noso is a new generation of peer-to-peer digital currency completely build from the ground up that pioneers a novel, fairer distribution of coins through PoPW and PoS emission and a more democratic voting system. Noso is open-source, not owned by anyone (unlicensed) and provides a unique democratic voting system through GVTs. Noso also aims to be more resource efficient than many of its predecessors replacing the long held maxim of one-CPU-one-vote with the idea of one-IP4-one-vote.
