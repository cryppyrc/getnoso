Noso is a simple, fast, and powerful currency available to anyone and trusted by many.

Low transaction fees

Unlike many other digital currencies, Noso has fixed transaction fees. Every transaction costs exatcly 0.01% of the total transaction volume. The fixed fee makes it extremely easy to predict transaction costs for merchants and allows Noso to scale indefinintely.

Fast international payments

Sending Noso across continents is as easy as sending them to the person sitting next to you. There are no transaction limits, waiting times, or extra fees for international transfers. With Noso merchants can request near-instant payments from anywhere on the globe.

Eco-friendly

With the emerging climate crisis businesses are searching for greener technologies. Noso aims to be more eco-friendly than most other digital currencies by utilising a novel verification scheme; part PoPW and PoS making wasteful mining farms a story of the past.
