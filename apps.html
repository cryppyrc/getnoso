Desktop Wallet

The Desktop Wallet allows you to generate, import, export, delete, or lock (password protect) individual addresses. It also allows you to send and receive Noso and keeps a running total of all addresses in your wallet. Learn more in the Docs.

Mobile Wallet

The Mobile Wallet allows you to generate, import, export, delete, or lock (password protect) individual addresses. It also allows you to send and receive Noso and keeps a running total of all addresses in your wallet. Learn more in the Docs.

Download

Nosoearn

Nosoearn is Noso's native PoPW optimised CLI app that makes earning Nosos a breeze. Nosoearn was introduced in Nov. 2022 as successor of the deprecated Consominer PoW mining app. Learn more in the Docs.

Download

Mobile Earner

Due to its unique PoPW mining algorithm, Noso is especially suitable to be mined on small, energy efficient devices - placing Noso at the forefront of climate friendly crypto currencies. The mobile miner allows anyone with an Android device to mine Noso with a click of a button. Learn more in the Docs.

Download

Consonode

Noso's coin emission scheme is part PoPW and part PoS. The PoS part is managed by Nodes that require the user to stake Noso whereby securing the mainnet. Consonode is currently under development and a release is expected soon. In the meantime Nodes are operated through the end-of-lifecycle NosoWallet app.
