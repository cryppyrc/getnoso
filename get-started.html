Get started with Noso in a three simple steps.

Inform yourself

Noso is different from your payment system you are used to. Before you start using Noso, we encourage you to do your own research by reading our white paper and get to know the community and ask questions.

Read more Connect on Discord

Choose your wallet

You will need to select a wallet in order to hold and trade Noso. Like regular physical wallets, a Noso wallet functions as a place to store your assets and enables you to receive aor spend Noso.

Choose a Wallet Connect on Discord

Get Noso

Noso can be earned through a unique and fair distribution mechanism called POPW as well as traded on exchanges. Join us on Discord to learn more.

Earn Noso Connect on Discord
