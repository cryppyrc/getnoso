Noso is a simple, fast, and powerful currency available to anyone and trusted by many.

Fair Distribution

Noso was released without a pre-mine. Development funds are obtained through donation. In addition, Noso's new and unique PoPW algorithm guarantees a fair eco-friendly distribution of coins among miners - fending off large mining farms.

Passive Income

Node operators receive a reward for securing the network. Over time the percentage of Noso emitted through PoPW will gradually decrease and consequently PoS rewards will increase meaning that hosting a Node will provide passive income with time.

Have your say

Noso is not controlled by a single person or group. Instead, anyone can invest into GVTs that grants the holder a voice during important project decisions. This way all decisions are truely democratic and made in the best interest of the project and its stakeholders.

Low transaction fees

Unlike many other digital currencies, Noso has fixed transaction fees. Every transaction costs exatcly 0.01% of the total transaction volume. The fixed fee makes it extremely easy to predict transaction costs and allows Noso to scale indefinintely.

Pay anywhere

Receiving and sending Noso on the go is as simple as can be. Noso does not know jurisdictional or territorial boundaries and allows you to transfer values across the globe in an instance. Noso mainnet is always running and never sleeps, even on weekends and holidays.

Protect your Privacy

In order to send or receive Noso, you are not required to disclose your identity. In fact all you need to know is the Noso address of the party you wish to trade with. Note: If privacy is a serious concern, additional efforts may be required from your side to protect your identity.
